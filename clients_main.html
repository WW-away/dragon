Exhibitions

Dragon Fine Art engages actively in the logistics of organized art exhibitions throughout the country and abroad. Whether the exhibition is in an art museum, a gallery, a trade show site, a public institution or even a shopping mall, Dragon Fine Art simply makes the import/export, installation/deinstallation a smooth experience that the clients can actually enjoy.

Art Museums

From the early planning stage, Dragon Fine Art lends professional advice to art museums to help them better organize the logistics schedule of their project. A designated coordinator follows up on the project from the beginning to the end to ensure that the service we deliver lives up to the promise we make.

Galleries

Dragon Fine Art offers the most cost-effective solutions to galleries while maintaining the maximum flexibility on the operation. All this is to help galleries promote their sales, where all business root.

Art fairs

Dragon Fine Art is a seasoned art fair logistics provider, with 5 consecutive years of experience serving as the official customs broker and shipping agent for ART021, one of the two large scale contemporary art fairs in Shanghai. Participating galleries are so satisfied with the service and dedication that they consign to Dragon more shipment even after the fair.

Corporate & Private clients

Dragon Fine Art understands the different needs of its corporate & private clients and provides bespoke services to any occasion desired: a private collection exhibition, a new house decoration piece installation, an auctioned piece importation, or an office center piece swapping etc.
