OUR MISSION

Founded in 2015 in Shanghai, Dragon Fine Art is now a well-known name across China in the field of fine art packing and moving. The company strives to deliver high-level service to the clients at an affordable price.

Dragon Fine Art serves clients ranging from Exhibitions, Art Museums to Art Fairs, Galleries, Corporate & Private collectors. Domestic or international, single piece or multiple work group, Dragon Fine Art offers total peace-of-mind fine art moving solutions backboned by a seasoned team and an extensive agent network worldwide.
